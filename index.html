Simply Music

Simply Music is an app that allows musicians to explore more option during music composition to create more intersting music. That gives musicians that ability to quickly invoke substitutions and inversions to chords in a quick and manageble way.

Music Theory Logic

The most difficult part of this app was figuring out how to get my logic to act in accordance with music theory. I didn't use an API within this app but instead made a library of all possible notes in music to draw from and formulate chords and templates
